About VibeKE

VibeKE is a community-driven Kenyan entertainment platform built to help you discover the best of Kenyan movies, creators and artists. Whether you're feeling happy, sad, hype or chill — we've got something for your mood.

Built by a Kenyan, for Kenyans.

Our Mission

To make Kenyan entertainment easy to discover and celebrate the talent coming out of Kenya every day.

Our Vision

A platform where every Kenyan can find something to watch, listen to, or follow — all in one place.

Community First

VibeKE is powered by the community. Every recommendation you add makes the platform better for everyone.
